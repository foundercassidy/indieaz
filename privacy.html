Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Who we are

indie AZ is an independent community project operated by a private Arizona resident. It is not a registered business entity, not a corporation, and not affiliated with any other company, brand, or commercial enterprise. indie AZ has no commercial affiliation with any business, shop, or organization listed or unlisted on this site.

indie AZ is not for profit. No goods or services are sold through this site. No transactions are facilitated. This is a directory, a community project, and an act of paying attention.

When we say "we," "us," or "indie AZ," we mean the individual operating this project. We collect only the information we genuinely need to run this directory and communicate with the people who use it. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. We do not share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Information we collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly, and basic technical information collected automatically when you use the site.

Information you provide:

  • Email address when you join our Zine or waitlist
  • Shop name, owner name, email, phone, address, and business details when you submit a shop for consideration
  • Any additional information you choose to include in a submission or message to us

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic analytics data such as page views, referral sources, and browser type
  • IP address for security and fraud prevention purposes

How we use your information

  • To review and respond to shop submission applications
  • To send indie AZ communications to people who have opted in via our Zine signup
  • To maintain the directory and communicate with listed shop owners about their listing
  • To improve the site and understand how people use it

Email communications

If you join our Zine, you will receive periodic updates from indie AZ about new shops, directory news, and Arizona independent commerce. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. We do not send marketing emails on behalf of third parties.

Shop submissions

Information submitted as part of a shop application is used solely for the purpose of reviewing and responding to that application. If your shop is listed in the directory, your shop name, category, location, and contact details may be displayed publicly. Personal contact information for owners is never displayed publicly without your consent.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the site -- such as email delivery services and hosting providers -- but only as necessary to provide those services, and only under confidentiality agreements.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users or the public.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate the site. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. If we add analytics tools in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at info@indieaz.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. If you unsubscribe from our Zine, we remove your email from our mailing list promptly. Shop application data is retained for one year regardless of outcome.

Children

indie AZ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will note the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@indieaz.com.
